Rati Devidze

(რატი დევიძე*)

I am a final-year PhD student at the Machine Teaching group of Max Planck Institute for Software Systems: MPI-SWS, in Saarbrücken, Germany. I am also a member of the Saarbrücken Graduate School of Computer Science.

Throughout my tenure in Computer Science, I've been focused on advancing Reinforcement Learning methodologies. My primary research interests lie in formalizing reward design frameworks within the Reinforcement Learning framework. My research is to design informative and explicable reward functions, that provide clear signals to guide agents toward their goals by incentivizing optimal actions and discouraging incorrect ones via negative rewards.
